The Toxin

毒素

Find the Illegal Shipment in the final Risk of Rain stage storage room to unlock The Toxin.

Steps

  1. Route to the final Risk of Rain stage.
  2. Find a keycard from golden containers, enemies, or blast-door areas.
  3. Go to the storage room on the far right side of the ship before Providence.
  4. Use the keycard, then inspect/open the black hidden chests on the shelves until The Toxin is found.

Stages

  • Risk of Rain (variant any)

Sources

  • primagames.com
