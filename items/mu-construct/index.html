Mu Construct

Mu构造体

Find the Mu Construct through the hidden path in Desolate Forest Stage Variant #4.

Steps

  1. Route into Desolate Forest Stage Variant #4.
  2. Go to the left side and find the large green-moss cave.
  3. Exit the cave on the left, use the geyser to launch toward the yellow vine, then climb partway up.
  4. Jump right into the hidden wall opening and follow the long hidden path to the Mu Construct.

Stages

  • Desolate Forest (variant 4)
