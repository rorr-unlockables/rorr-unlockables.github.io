Boar Beach

野猪滩

Enter Boar Beach from Ancient Valley Stage Variant #2 through the upper-left fence.

Location

Secret stage from Ancient Valley Stage Variant #2

Steps

  1. Route into Ancient Valley Stage Variant #2.
  2. Go to the fence on the upper-left part of the stage.
  3. Press up at the fence to enter Boar Beach.
  4. After entering Boar Beach, walk left if you want to route toward the log or Acrid's Obsidian prism.

Stages

  • Ancient Valley (variant 2)

Sources
