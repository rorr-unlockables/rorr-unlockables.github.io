Kin

亲族

Break into the hidden area in Dried Lake and activate three buttons to reveal the Artifact of Kin.

Location

Somewhere Dry

Steps

  1. Route into Dried Lake, Stage Variant #1, #2, or #3.
  2. Go to the far right side of the stage and find the breakable wall.
  3. Break the wall with a suitable Survivor skill. Hopoo Feather, Rusty Jetpack, or Photon Jetpack may be needed to reach it.
  4. Activate all three buttons in the puzzle room.
  5. Pick up the Artifact of Kin at the Artifact Portal.

Stages

  • Dried Lake (variant 1, 2, 3)

Sources

  • Risk of Rain Returns Wiki: Dried Lake / Artifact of Kin
