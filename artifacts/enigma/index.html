Enigma

谜团

Use the hidden bottom-right cove route in Sunken Tombs to reach and pick up the Artifact of Enigma.

Location

Drowned Below

Steps

  1. Route into Sunken Tombs, Stage Variant #1, #2, or #3.
  2. Go to the bottom-right of the map and find the drop-down into a cove.
  3. Move right through the cove route.
  4. Use the hidden geyser boost to reach the tunnel.
  5. Follow the tunnel to the end and pick up the Artifact of Enigma.

Stages

  • Sunken Tombs (variant 1, 2, 3)

Sources

  • Risk of Rain Returns Wiki: Artifact of Enigma
