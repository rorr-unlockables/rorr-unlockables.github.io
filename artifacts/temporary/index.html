Tempus

须臾

Reach Temple of the Elders before 16 minutes and pick up the Artifact of Tempus before it disappears.

Location

Entropy

Steps

  1. Route into Temple of the Elders on any stage variant.
  2. Reach the stage before 16 minutes have passed in the run.
  3. Pick up the Artifact of Tempus before it disappears.
  4. If Temple of the Elders is reached after 16 minutes, the artifact will not appear.

Stages

  • Temple of the Elders (variant any)

Sources

  • Risk of Rain Returns Wiki: Temple of the Elders / Artifact of Tempus
