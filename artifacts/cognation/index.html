artifact

Cognation

相性

Activate two buttons in Desolate Forest, defeat the spawned Desecrated Rock Golem, then pick up the Artifact of Cognation.

Location

Seeing Ghosts

Steps

  1. Route into Desolate Forest, Stage Variant #4, on Modern ruleset.
  2. Find the hidden vine on the right side of the stage.
  3. Activate both puzzle buttons.
  4. Defeat the spawned Desecrated Rock Golem.
  5. Pick up the Artifact of Cognation at the Artifact Portal.

Stages

  • Desolate Forest (variant 4)

Sources
