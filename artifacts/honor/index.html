Honor

荣耀

Find and pick up the Artifact of Honor in Desolate Forest.

Location

In The Forest

Steps

  1. Route into Desolate Forest, Stage Variant #1, #2, or #3.
  2. On Stage Variant #3, check the top-left side of the map, to the right of the waterfall.
  3. Look for a blue root in the floor and use the hidden path there to reach the artifact.
  4. Pick up the artifact object.

Stages

  • Desolate Forest (variant 1, 2, 3)

Sources

  • Risk of Rain Returns Wiki: Desolate Forest / Artifact of Honor
