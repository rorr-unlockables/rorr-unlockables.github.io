Distortion

扭曲

Press the hidden button in Damp Caverns to spawn the Artifact of Distortion on a nearby pedestal.

Location

Among the Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Route into Damp Caverns, Stage Variant #1, #2, or #3.
  2. Find the artifact button. On Stage Variant #2, the button is at the marked artifact-button location.
  3. Press the button.
  4. Go to the pedestal to the left of the button location and pick up the spawned artifact.

Stages

  • Damp Caverns (variant 1, 2, 3)

Sources

  • Risk of Rain Returns Wiki: Damp Caverns / Artifact of Distortion
