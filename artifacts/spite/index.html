artifact

Spite

恶意

Use a Parent enemy to break the fissure in Sky Meadow, then complete the hidden obstacle course.

Location

In the Meadow

Steps

  1. Route into Sky Meadow, Stage Variant #1, #2, or #3.
  2. Near the center of the stage, find the small crack in the ground.
  3. Lead a Parent enemy to the crack and make it smash the fissure until it breaks.
  4. Enter the newly opened hole below the map.
  5. Complete the hidden obstacle course and pick up the Artifact of Spite.

Stages

  • Sky Meadow (variant 1, 2, 3)

Sources

  • Risk of Rain Returns Wiki: Sky Meadow / Artifact of Spite
