Dissonance

纷争

Find 4 hidden Artifact Shards to unlock Artifact of Dissonance.

Location

Sight-Seeing

Steps

  1. Collect the shard in Ancient Valley variants #4-#6: it is on the right side of the central mountain and can be reached by a hidden chain or rope at the top of the mountain.
  2. Collect the shard in Sunken Tombs Stage Variant #5.
  3. Collect the shard in Magma Barracks variants #4-#6; on Stage Variant #6, check near the top-right of the map.
  4. Collect the shard in Hive Cluster variants #1-#3; on Stage Variant #1, check the bottom-right area near the Bloated Survivor.
  5. After 4 total Artifact Shards are collected, Artifact of Dissonance unlocks.

Stages

  • Ancient Valley (variant 4, 5, 6)
  • Sunken Tombs (variant 5)
  • Magma Barracks (variant 4, 5, 6)
  • Hive Cluster (variant 1, 2, 3)
