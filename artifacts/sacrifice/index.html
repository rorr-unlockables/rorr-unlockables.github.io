Sacrifice

牺牲

Shoot three buttons hidden behind walls in Magma Barracks to unlock the Artifact of Sacrifice.

Location

Near the Core

Steps

  1. Route into Magma Barracks Stage Variant #1, #2, or #3 on any ruleset.
  2. Search throughout the stage for three buttons hidden behind walls.
  3. Shoot all three hidden buttons. Evolved Lemurians can also hit the buttons, which helps characters that cannot attack through walls.
  4. Pick up the Artifact of Sacrifice after the button objective is completed.

Stages

  • Magma Barracks (variant 1, 2, 3)

artifact

牺牲

射击<熔岩要塞>中藏在墙后的三个按钮，以解锁<神器：牺牲>。

地点

核心附近

步骤

  1. 使用任意规则集进入<熔岩要塞>关卡变体 #1、#2 或 #3。
  2. 在整个关卡中搜索藏在墙后的三个按钮。
  3. 射击全部三个隐藏按钮。<飞天利莫里亚人>也能击中按钮，可帮助无法穿墙攻击的角色。
  4. 按钮目标完成后，拾取<神器：牺牲>。

关卡

  • 熔岩要塞 (变体 1, 2, 3)
