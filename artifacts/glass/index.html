Glass

玻璃

Complete the hidden timed button challenge in Ancient Valley to spawn the Artifact of Glass.

Location

Below the Bridge

Steps

  1. Route into Ancient Valley, Stage Variant #1, #2, or #3.
  2. Go to the left side of the map and find the cave hidden behind walls or ropes.
  3. Enter the platforming challenge area.
  4. Hit all 5 buttons spread through the challenge within 15 seconds.
  5. Pick up the spawned Artifact of Glass.

Stages

  • Ancient Valley (variant 1, 2, 3)

Sources

  • Risk of Rain Returns Wiki: Ancient Valley / Artifact of Glass
