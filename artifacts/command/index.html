Find and pick up the Artifact of Command in Hive Cluster.

Location

In the Hive

Steps

  1. Route into Hive Cluster, Stage Variant #1, #2, or #3.
  2. Go to the top-right area of the stage. On Stage Variant #1, the artifact is in the top-right corner.
  3. Pick up the Artifact of Command.
