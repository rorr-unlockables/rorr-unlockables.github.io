Strange Prism: Stasis

奇怪棱镜：静滞

Find Artificer's Stasis prism in Dried Lake Stage Variant #3.

Location

Stasis

Steps

  1. Route into Dried Lake Stage Variant #3.
  2. Go to the very top-left of the stage by the fishing skeleton.
  3. Hug the wall to fall out of bounds.
  4. Fall back in bounds immediately to land on a ledge at the bottom.
  5. Continue right on the secret ledge and search until the prism is found.

Stages

  • Dried Lake (variant 3)
