Strange Prism: Obsidian

奇怪棱镜：黑曜石

Reach Boar Beach from Ancient Valley Stage Variant #2, then platform to Acrid's Obsidian prism.

Location

Obsidian

Steps

  1. Route into Ancient Valley Stage Variant #2.
  2. Go to the fence on the upper-left part of the stage and press up to enter Boar Beach.
  3. Once in Boar Beach, immediately walk left.
  4. Climb down the secret rope to reach a hidden platform.
  5. Continue platforming to the right.
  6. Collect the prism.

Stages

  • Boar Beach (variant 2)
  • Ancient Valley (variant 2)

Sources
