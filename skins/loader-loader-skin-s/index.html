Strange Prism: Nebula

奇怪棱镜：星云

Find Loader's Nebula prism after the Artifact of Spite obstacle course in Sky Meadow Stage Variant #2.

Location

Nebula

Steps

  1. Route into Sky Meadow Stage Variant #2.
  2. Open and complete the obstacle course used to reach the Artifact of Spite.
  3. After the course, continue left and upward.
  4. Collect the prism.

Stages

  • Sky Meadow (variant 2)
