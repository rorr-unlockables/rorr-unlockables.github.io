Strange Prism: Pyrite

奇怪棱镜：硫化铁

Find the Commando Pyrite prism in Desolate Forest Stage Variant #3.

Location

Pyrite

Steps

  1. Route into Desolate Forest Stage Variant #3.
  2. Go to the far left side of the map.
  3. Find the blue root in the floor.
  4. Climb down through it to reach a small alcove.
  5. Collect the prism.

Stages

  • Desolate Forest (variant 3)

Sources
