Strange Prism: Malice

奇怪棱镜：恶意

Find Mercenary's Malice prism in Risk of Rain Stage Variant #2.

Location

Malice

Steps

  1. Route to Risk of Rain Stage Variant #2.
  2. Go through the hole in the cabin wall.
  3. Jump down the cliff and enter the cave in the cliffside.
  4. Pass through the cave and the party area.
  5. Jump up into the secret tunnel.
  6. Continue through the tunnel until the prism is found on the last pillar.

Stages

  • Risk of Rain (variant 2)

Sources

  • Risk of Rain Returns Wiki: Risk of Rain / Mercenary Prism
